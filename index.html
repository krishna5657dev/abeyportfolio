About

Sound Engineer & Production Mixer

Based in Kerala, I specialize in Sound Design, Production Mixing, and Foley to bring films, television, and OTT productions to life.

I am a creative and detail-driven Sound Designer and Foley Specialist with 8 years of experience in film, television, and OTT productions. I have collaborated with global brands like Netflix and Red Bull, as well as leading regional studios across India and the UAE. I am skilled in production sound, Foley artistry, ADR, and immersive mixing.

As the founder of NomadicBeatz_ EFX Studios in Kochi, I combine technical mastery with artistic instinct to deliver high-quality audio from on-set capture to the final mix, crafting powerful soundscapes that elevate storytelling.
